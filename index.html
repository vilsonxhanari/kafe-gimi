Shtepia e Kafe Gimi

A tradition that connects..

Nestled within the charming bulevaord of our historical city, you'll find "Coffee House Gimi"- a coffee shop and company that is a true testament to the city's rich past and its contemporary coffee culture.

A family tradition from coffee roasting to serving...

Nestled in the heart of the city, Coffee House Gimi is charming coffee oasis where the aroma of freshly roasted beans greet you at the door. Whether you are seeking a peaceful corner for quiet contemplation or a lively space to catch up with friends, Coffee House Gimi invites you to relax, sip and savor the simple pleasures of live, one cup at a time"
